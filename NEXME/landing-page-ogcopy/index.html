TRANSFORMING REAL ESTATE

NEXME was born out of the challenges that plague homebuyers and sellers today. If you’ve ever been shopping for a home, you know what we’re talking about. You see a real estate sign on a home, and you want to see it right away. Or you’re online and see a home you love and want to see it now. You have to go through the cumbersome process of calling a real estate agent, leaving a message, waiting around for someone to get back to you, wasting precious time, and maybe losing out on the home of your dreams. All while it still being expensive to buy and sell. Home sellers face similar challenges, with trying to sell their homes as quickly as possible.

About Us

NEXME started out of the frustration we saw in the home buying and selling process. As other industries accelerated their transformation, real estate remained stagnant. Time and time again, we listened to homebuyers and sellers complain about the lengthy process of buying and selling a home and how expensive commission fees were. We tackled a few different problems before realizing that accelerating the process was what homebuyers and sellers wanted. In today’s on-demand world, instant access is necessary. NEXME wants to provide that instant access to homebuyers and sellers everywhere at a fraction of the cost.
